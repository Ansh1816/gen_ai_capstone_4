# SMS and Call Reminder Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the standard approach for sending appointment reminders to patients via SMS, phone call, and email to reduce appointment no-shows and improve patient engagement.

## SMS Reminder Schedule
1. **First Reminder (48 hours)**: Automated SMS sent 48 hours before the appointment with date, time, location, and provider name.
2. **Second Reminder (24 hours)**: For medium-risk and high-risk patients, a personalized SMS is sent 24 hours before the appointment.
3. **Day-of Reminder (Morning)**: Optional same-day morning SMS for afternoon appointments.

## SMS Content Guidelines
- Include: Patient first name, appointment date/time, clinic location, provider name.
- Include a reply option (e.g., "Reply CONFIRM to confirm or CANCEL to cancel").
- Keep SMS under 160 characters where possible.
- Use professional but friendly tone.
- Do not include sensitive medical information in SMS.

## Phone Call Protocol
- **When Required**: For all high-risk patients and medium-risk patients who do not respond to SMS.
- **Timing**: Calls should be made 24–48 hours before the appointment.
- **Script**: Staff should follow the standardized call script including patient identification, appointment confirmation, and offer to reschedule.
- **Voicemail**: If the patient does not answer, leave a voicemail requesting callback. Attempt a second call within 4 hours.
- **Maximum Attempts**: Up to 3 call attempts before documenting as "unable to reach."

## Email Reminders
- Sent as a supplement to SMS for patients who have provided email addresses.
- Include calendar attachment (.ics) for easy addition to personal calendars.
- Include directions, parking information, and pre-appointment instructions.

## Patient Preferences
- Patients should be able to opt into their preferred communication channel.
- Respect "Do Not Call" and "Do Not Text" preferences.
- HIPAA-compliant messaging at all times.

## Effectiveness Metrics
- SMS open/response rate target: >60%
- Call connection rate target: >70%
- No-show rate reduction target after reminder implementation: 15–25% improvement
